    WXJOURNEY

    WXJourney allows you to create a custom weather forecast by date and location.

    How to use:

    Add record(s) Enter a location using the [location picker]. Select a single date or date range in the [date picker]. Press [+] to create the record(s).
    Refresh forecast Use [refresh] to update the forecasts. New forecast data is available at 00:00, 06:00, 12:00, and 18:00 UTC.
    Change Temperature Unit Tap [°F °C] to toggle the displayed unit.
    Edit a record Change the date of an existing record by pressing the [date picker]. Records automatically sort chronologically.
    Delete a record Press [X] in an existing record to delete the record.

    Forecast data is provided by the National Weather Service (NWS) and the European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF).

    NWS date is available for 7 days from the current date and is used for locations within the U.S.

    ECMWF data is available for 15 days from the current date and is used for locations outside of the U.S. or when NWS data is not available.

    The temperatures displayed for AM, PM, and EV are the predicted temperatures for 8AM, 2PM, and 8PM respectively.

    The weather conditions displayed are for a range of six hours after the start of the respective time period.
